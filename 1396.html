Chicken and Dumping Casserole

created: 2014-02-22 12:31:29

servings: 8

recipe:

Ingredients 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded ( I use a store bought rotisserie chicken) 2 cups chicken broth Â½ stick of butter 2 cups Bisquick 2 cups whole milk 1 can cream of chicken soup (the herbed cream of chicken soup if you can find it) 3 teaspoons of chicken granules (I use Wylers) Â½ teaspoon dried sage 1 teaspoon black pepper Â½ teaspoon of salt or more to taste Directions Directions: Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Layer 1 - In 9 x 13 casserole dish, melt 1/2 stick of butter. Spread shredded chicken over butter. Sprinkle black pepper and dried sage over this layer. Do not stir. Layer 2 - In small bowl, mix milk and Bisquick. Slowly pour all over chicken. Do not stir. Layer 3 - In medium bowl, whisk together 2 cups of chicken broth, chicken granules and soup. Once blended, slowly pour over the Bisquick layer. Do not Stir. Bake casserole for 30-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown
