Betty's Pound Cake

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-27 20:13:03

servings:

notes:

This is my sister's mother- in- law's recipe. Yummy!

recipe:

3 cups flour 1 cup Crisco 4 eggs (warm) 1 cup buttermilk 3 cups flour 1 tb vanilla 1/4 t salt 1/3 t baking soda Put in a greased and floured tube cake pan Put in a cold oven and set at 350. Should be ready in an hour
