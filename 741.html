DeeDee's Meatloaf

author: texaskadi

created: 2011-01-21 12:58:18

servings: 8

notes:

recipe:

2 lbs. Ground meat 1/2 c. chopped onion 3/4 c. fresh breadcrumbs 1 beaten egg 1/2 can (8 oz.) tomato sauce 2 tsps salt 1/4 tsp pepper 1/2 tsp Worchestire Sauce Mix above ingredients into meat loaf. Sauce: 1/2 can (the other 1/2 of the above 8 oz.) tomato sauce 1/2 c. water 2 Tbsp vinegar 1/4 c. brown sugar 1 tsp worchestire sauce 1 tsp dry mustard Mix and our over meat loaf. Bake at 300 degreesfor 1-1/2 hours
