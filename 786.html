Chicken Tortilla Casserole

author: texaskadi

created: 2011-01-21 14:02:27

servings: 6

notes:

recipe:

Debone chicken. Mix with 1 can cream of chicken, 1 can rotel,, and 1 can cream of mushroom soup. Add some sauteed onions. Warm over medium burner. Layer flour tortillas on bottom. They layer chicken, then tortilla, then chicken. Top with grated cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
