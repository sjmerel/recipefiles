Apricot Nectar Cake

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-23 18:43:47

servings:

notes:

Tommie Morris's recipe

recipe:

1 box yellow cake mix 3/4 cup wesson oil 4 eggs, add one at a time 1 teaspoon of vanilla 3/4 cup apricot nectar 1 box lemon jello 1 t. lemon juice Mix above ingredients. Bake at 325 degrees 55 minutes in greased stem pan. Frosting; Mix1/4 cup of apricot nectar and 1/2 cup powdered sugar. Pour over cake while hot.
