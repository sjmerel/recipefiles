Marinated Pork Loin

author: ksvoboda

created: 2006-07-04 20:45:34

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1/2 cup soy sauce 1/4 cup cold water 2 cloves garlic 1 tblsp. grated onion 1 tblsp. white vinegar 1/4 tsp. red pepper Marinate pork loin for 8 hours. Bake at 325 until done. Mustard Sauce: 1/3 cup mayo 1/3 cup sour cream 1 tblsp. dry mustard 1 tblsp. grated onion 1 tsp. white vinegar 1/4 tsp. salt
