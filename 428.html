recipe:

2 lbs. frozen hash brown potatoes 1 stick melted butter 1 cup chopped onion 8 on. sour cream l can cream of mushroom soup 1 tsp salt 1/2 stick of butter melted 2 1/2 cup of crushed corn flakes Mix first 7 ingredients in a 2 1/2 quart casserole. Mix remaining ingredients and put on top. Bake 1 1/2 hours at 350
