Quick Egg Noodle Lasagna

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-22 20:34:04

servings:

notes:

Mom's recipe

recipe:

1# wide noodles 2 tbs. cooking oil 2 tsp monsodium glutanate 1 # cottage cheese 1# ground beef 3 10 1/2 cans spaghetti sauce with mushrooms grated parmesan cheese Prepare noodles as directed on package. Sprinkle monsodium glutenate on beef then brown in oil. In casserole layer noodles, beef, and cottage cheese. Pour spghetti sauce on top and sprinkle with grated cheese. Bake 375 degrees for 20 minutes. Serve hot with additional grated cheese.
