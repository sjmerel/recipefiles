Tortilla Soup

author: myrnawis

created: 2012-01-24 11:53:45

servings: 8

notes:

Weight Watchers recipe

recipe:

2 cans chicken broth 3 cans dices tomatoes 1 large onion 1 Jalapeno pepper diced 2 cloves garlic 2 Tbls cumin powder 2 Tbls worcestershire sauce 2 teas. chili powder black pepper to taste 4 flour tortillas Simmer 1 hour. When dishing up soup cut up 1/2 od flour tortilla shell into strips and put in each bowl. if desired, you can top with shredded cheese.
