Mom's Chop Suey

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-22 20:26:32

servings:

notes:

From my mom's recipe file, Jeanne Ellis

recipe:

1 1/2 pound chicken 2 cans mushroom soup 1 can cream of chicken soup 1 cup chow mein noodles 1 cup chopped onion 1/2 cup chopped celery 1 cup uncooked rice 1/4 cup soy sauce 3 3/4 cup water Cover chicken with water. Cook on stove til done. Cool and debone. Mix together chicken, soups, onion celery and rice. Stir in1/4 soy sauce, and 3 3/4 water. Stir in noodles. 350 degrees 1 hour. Stir once or twice
