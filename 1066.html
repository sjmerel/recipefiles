recipe:

1 bag coleslaw w/ carrots Mix together the following: 1c, miracle whip 1/2 c sugar 1 tbls. vinegar 1/2 c sunflower seeds real bacon bits chopped broccoli & cauliflower 1 c shredded cheese 1/3 flavored almonds (slivered) 1/2 c black olives 1 tomato Combine all together
