My Chili

author: barfirst

created: 2010-01-03 18:29:38

servings: 8

notes:

recipe:

2 lbs ground beef 1 can tomato paste 2 green peppers 1 19 oz can kidney beans 1 lrg onion 1 sml can chopped chillies 1 16 oz medium picante sauce 3 cloves garlic 4 sticks celery 1 20 oz can whole peeled tomatos 3 T chili powder 1 T salt 2 T sugar 1 jalepeno 1 t ground cumin Saute' beef, onion, garlic, peppers, celery, jalepeno add rest and stir (tomatos w/o juice) add beans w/ juice simmer 90 minutes
