French Onion Chicken

author: LadyT

created: 2007-08-21 02:39:09

servings: 4-6

notes:

For Summer: Serve with Fresh Tomato & Avocado Salad Recipe For Fall: Combine with Sweet Potato Bake. For Winter:Make a Chicken Mushroom Onion Casserole For a Light Meal: Salad with Chicken Recipe For a Lunch or Brunch: Chicken Salad Delish Recipe

recipe:

Preheat Oven 375 Gather: Large Package Chicken Tenders 1 Tub of French Onion Dip Italian Bread Crumbs w/ Parmesan Cheese/Salt/Pepper/Herbs * Olive Oil Then: ~Put Olive Oil in a Skillet on Medium Heat ~Clean/Dry Chicken, Dip into French Onion Dip, Then, Bread Crumb Mixture ~Saute each side on Medium Heat in Olive Oil to a golden brown @ 3-4 minutes ~Drain on paper towel ~Bake on a cookie sheet additional @ 8-10 minutes (just until it is cooked through) ~Salt & Enjoy! *I use Aldi's spice club seasoning w/ grinder- chicken- ingredients: whole black peppercorns, mustard seeds, red & green bell pepper pieces, onion, coarse sea salt, garlic.
