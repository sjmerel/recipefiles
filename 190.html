Sesame Farfalle

author: laura

created: 2005-01-17 00:34:15

servings: 4

notes:

Goes well with the ginger chicken.

recipe:

1/2 -3/4 lb. farfalle 1 tsp. sesame oil (or more to taste) 1 thinly sliced scallion 1/8 tsp. kosher salt 1/2 tsp. white sesame seeds. Cook the pasta. Mix with other ingredients in bowl.
