Oreo Yum Yum

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-29 14:27:54

servings:

notes:

Kids love this as well as adults Sauce can be saved for several weeks.

recipe:

Small pkg. oreos crumbled 1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream softened Stir these two ingredients together and put in 9x13 pan. Refreeze Sauce: 1 can Hershey syrup 1 can Eagle Brand milk 1 stick oleo Stir in saucepan until butter is melted. Cool. Cut ice cream into squares. Pour warm chocolate sauce over before serving. Yum, yum
