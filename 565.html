Lime Cottage Cheese Salad

author: jmderusha

created: 2009-11-15 21:17:44

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 pkg. lime jello (small) dissolved in 1 c. hot water Drain 1 sm. can crushed pineapple. Use juice in jello cool jello to syrupy stage and add: 1 c. cottage cheese crushed pineapple already drained 1/2 c. miracle whip Add to jello and mix well. Set til firm
