Artichoke/Cheese Dip

author: debandrich

created: 2007-04-14 19:28:32

servings: 8 OR MORE

notes:

FROM TERRI EBEL

recipe:

1 16OZ HELLMANS MAYO 1 8OZ KRAFT PARMESON CHEESE 4 CUP SARGENTO (OR ANY BRAND) MOZZERALLA (CHEF STYLE) 2-JARS REESE MARINATED ANTICHOKS (DRAINED) MIX ALL INGREDIENTS TOGETHER BAKE AT 350, FOR 30MIN OR UNTIL BROWN ON TOP SERVE W/CRACKERS OR SCOOPS WHAT EVER YOU LIKE HAPPY EATING
