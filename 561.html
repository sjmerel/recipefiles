Bar B Q

author: GrandmaP

created: 2009-08-20 17:44:39

servings:

notes:

from Peg Willis

recipe:

1 1/2 lbs hamburger, 12 oz corned beef, 14 oz catsup, 1/3 cup dark brown sugar, garlic salt, 2 tbs chili powder, 8 of water and 1 med onion. Brown meat and drain. add rest of ingredients and simmer at least 1 hour.
