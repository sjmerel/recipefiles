Mixed Baby Greens Salad with Mandarin

author: ksvoboda

created: 2006-07-04 20:48:12

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 bag mixed baby greens 10 oz. 1 can mandrin oranges drained 1/4 english or european seedless cucumber, thinly sliced 2 tblsp. rice wine or white vinegar 2 tsp. sugar 3 tblsp vegetable oil 1 tsp. toasted sesame oil Sesame seeds for garnish Salt and freshly ground pepper Combine greens, oranges and cucumber in salad bowl. In a small bowl, combine vinegar and suage. Whisk in vegetable oil and seamae oil. Pour dressing over salad. Add sesame seeds, salt and pepper and toss.
