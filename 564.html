Peanut Butter and Choc. Oatmeal Cookies

author: jmderusha

created: 2009-11-15 21:10:19

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 1/2 c. peanut butter 1 c. sugar 1/2 c pkd. brown sugar 1/2 c (1 stick) butter (softened) 2 t. vanilla 2 t. bkg. soda 3 eggs 4 c. snoqualmie oatmeal (heavy oatmeal) 1 c. choc. chips (or mini m & m's) Using an electric mixer, cream together peanut butter, sugars, butter, vanilla. Add eggs and continue to beat mixture until blended. Stir in oatmeal and choc. chips until blended. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased cookie sheets Bake 8-12 min. Makes 5 doz.
