Chipotle Chicken with Sweet Peppers

author: texaskadi

created: 2011-01-17 13:23:00

servings: 4

notes:

Serve this spicy dish with mashed potatoes.

recipe:

1 pound boneless chicken breasts 1 pound frozen pepper stir-fry 1 (14 oz). can stewed tomatoes, undrained 1 chipotle chili pepper in adobo sauce, minced,about 1 tablespoon) 1 teaspoon ground cumin 1/2 teaspoon salt 1 tablespoon olive oil Combine all ingredients except oil in a deep skillet or Dutch oven; bring to a boil over high heat. Reduce heat, cover tightly and simmer until chicken is no longer pink in center. 15 to 20 minutes. Remove hcicken, let cool and cut into chunks. Increase heat to medium-high; boil vegetables 15 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in olive oil. Pour vegetable mixture evenly over chicken.
