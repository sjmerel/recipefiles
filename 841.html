Quick Pepper Steak

author: texaskadi

created: 2011-06-06 12:13:35

servings: 4

notes:

This is a very tasty pepper steak which can be served as an independent entree of served wrapped in flour tortillas.

recipe:

Cut 1 lb sirloin steak into strips Green Pepper striips Red pepper strips Onion rings 2 tsp. parsley 1 tsp garlic powder Velvetta Cheese Cook steak strips in skillet with olive oil 8-10 minutes. Add next 5ingredients and cook 5 minutes more. Stir in 1 lb.of Velnetta cut into cubes. Cook on low until Velvetta is melted and blended.
