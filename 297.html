oriental cole slaw

author: debandrich

created: 2007-04-14 19:20:33

servings: 8 or more

notes:

Carol Boyer gave us this recipe and it is AWESOME

recipe:

Oriental Cole Slaw Salad Mix: 16oz Cole Slaw Mix, I use Dole's 2 Pkg Ramen Beef Noodles (uncooked) 6-7 Green Onions ( at least 2 inches of green leaf part also) Chopped 1 Cup toasted slivered or sliced Almonds 1 Cup Sunflower Kernels (salted or unsalted, I use salted) Mix well above ingredients together Refrigerate and add Salad Dressing Mix when ready or Add Salad Dressing and Refrigerate until ready to serve. I mix at least two hours ahead. I like the noodles to have time to absorb the Salad Dressing Salad Dressing Mix: 1 Cup Canola Oil 1/2 Cup Gran. sugar 1/3 Cup Cider Vinegar 2 pkg flavoring Mix from Beef Ramen Noodles Blend well above ingredients until smooth ( all sugar dissolved) Pour Dressing over Salad Mix & Stir in well. Serve immediately or refrigerate 2-3 hours first. Enjoy!
