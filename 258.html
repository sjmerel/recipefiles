Noodle Salad

author: ksvoboda

created: 2006-07-04 20:17:25

servings:

notes:

recipe:

2 pks Romane Noodles -Chicken Flavor 1 package slaw 1/2 cup slivered almonds 1/2 cup sunflower seeds 5 green onions chopped Mix slaw, broken up noodles, almonds, sunflowers and onions 1/3 cup vinegar 1/2 cup oil 1/2 cup sugar 2 chicken flavor paks from Romane Noodles Mix and pour over slaw.
