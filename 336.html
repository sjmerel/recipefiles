Fried Rice

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-06-05 17:28:37

servings:

notes:

recipe:

2 tablespoons chopped onion 1/2 cup canned mushroom stems and pieces, drained 1/2 cup finely chopped cooked chicken, pork, or ham 2 tablespoons cooking oil 4 cups cold cooked rice 2 tablespoons soy sauce 1 egg, beaten 2 tablespoons minced parsley 1/8 teaspoon pepper sautee onion, mushrooms and meat in oil in large skillet add rice adn soy sauce cook over low heat for 5-10 minutes add egg, parsley, and pepper cook additional 5 minutes
