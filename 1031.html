Good Chili

author: myrnawis

created: 2012-01-24 12:17:56

servings: 8

notes:

Weight Watchers recipe - 2cups = 3 points

recipe:

1 pound ground turkey breast meat 2 large onions 6 stalks celery 1 red pepper 1 green pepper 4 beef bouillon cubes 2 teas garlic salt 1 1/2 teas chili powder 3 teas cumin 8 ounces chili beans 15 ounce tomato sauce 1 1/2 quarts tomatoes Add all ingredients and simmer for 30 minutes
