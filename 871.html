Holiday Meatballs

author: texaskadi

created: 2011-06-06 13:20:20

servings: 10 - 12

notes:

For ameal servemeatballsover rice with cooked vegetables. You may also prepare in a slow cooker on high for 2 hours.

recipe:

1 can whole berry cranberry sauce 1 bottle chili sauce 1/4 cup orange marmalade 2 Tbsp soy sauce 2 Tbsp red wine vinegar 1 tsp crushed red pepper 32 oz. package frozen meatballs Stir together the first 6 ingrerdients. Cook, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes or until smooth. Add meatballs and cook 20 to 25 minutes or until thorooughly heated.
