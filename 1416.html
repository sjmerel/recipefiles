INCREDIBLE BREAKFAST BAKE

author: texaskadi

created: 2014-06-29 11:41:57

servings: 6

notes:

Fantastic for ANY meal!! (Share so ya don't lose it)

recipe:

1 Can Flaky Grands Bag shredded cheddar 8 oz. Half cup milk Cubed ham or ground cooked sausage 1 cup cooked 5 eggs Salt and pepper Add all ingredients and mix saving biscuits for last. Cut biscuits into fours and carefully mix in. Transfer to roomy casserole dish that has been oiled. Cook 25 at 350. You can't mess this up and it comes out looking JUST like picture. Serve for breakfast or dinner w small salad.
