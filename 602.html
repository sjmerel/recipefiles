Nancy's Punch

author: Half_Fast_Knitters

created: 2010-02-02 19:18:11

servings: 1 lg punch bowl

notes:

recipe:

1 - 24oz Bottle of Champagne 3 Liters dry white wine 2 -32 oz ginger ale Strawberries sliced some to float in punch. Strawberries sliced to put in an Ice Ring. Freeze Chill all then put in Punch Bowl. Add Ice ring with strawberries and strawberries to the punch. Enjoy!!
