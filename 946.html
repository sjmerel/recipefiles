Breakfast Cassarole

author: jmderusha

created: 2011-10-06 17:59:28

servings:

notes:

recipe:

8 hash brown squares 4 cups cheese-grated 2 cups sausage (1 roll of Jimmy Dean- browned mix together: 7 eggs 1 cup milk 1/2 t. salt 1/2 t. powdered mustard 1/2 t. pepper Grease pan- Layer: hash browns, cheese, meat, then egg mix. Cover with foil Bake 350 degrees 1 hour Uncover, and bake for 15 more minutes.
