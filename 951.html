created: 2011-10-06 18:01:33

recipe:

1 lb. ground turkey 1 chopped onion-optional brown and add: 1 envelope taco seasoning 1 ranch dressing envelope 3/4 c. water 2 cans stewed tomatoes or diced 15-16 oz. size 2 cans pinto beans 1 can corn Simmer about 15 minutes until hot through.
