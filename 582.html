Marinated Broccoli

author: gretchenlewis

created: 2009-11-21 21:45:26

servings:

notes:

Cut broccoli. Make marinade. Pour marinade over broccoli, store overnight in refrigerator. Stir or shake and then drain before serving.

recipe:

1 bunch broccoli - raw Marinade: 1/2 c vinegar 3/4 c oil 1/2 T sugar 1/2 T dill weed 1/2 T Accent 1/2 t salt pepper garlic salt
