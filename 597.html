Corn Bread

author: barfirst

created: 2010-01-03 18:13:14

servings: 4-9

notes:

recipe:

1 C flour 1 C yellow corn meal 1/2 tsp salt 1/2 C sugar 4 tsp baking powder 2 eggs 1 C milk 1/3 C melted butter sift together all dry ingredients mix butter, milk and eggs together, and mix into dry in 8x8x2 pan cook 425` 25-30 minutes
