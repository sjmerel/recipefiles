Waffles

author: lauraw

created: 2011-05-17 13:56:31

servings: 24

notes:

recipe:

6 C Brown Rice Flour 2 C WW Flour (or 1.5 more BR flour, 1/2 c Tapioca flour) 2 Tbsp Baking Powder 1 tsp Salt 3 Tbsp Cinnamon 4 C Hazelnut milk (or alternative) 3+ C Water 1/2 C Oil 6 Eggs Mix dry ingredients first, then add wet. Using cooking spray, spray waffle iron. Pour by ladleful onto iron. Cook 2 minutes a side. Remove, Eat or let cool and freeze for later. Appx: 300 calories each 11 g fat 47 g carbs 5 g protein
