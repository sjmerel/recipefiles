Sopapillo Cheesecake

author: texaskadi

created: 2011-01-17 12:39:57

servings: 10

notes:

This ia a wonderful dessert recipe shared with me by Gail Kerr.

recipe:

2 cans refrigerated crescent dinner rolls 16 unces creamcheese 1-1/2 sticks margarine 2 cups sugar 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg Instructions: Layer one can of crescent rolls crosswise in an 11 X 14 baking pan Mix 2 - 8 0z. packages cream cheese and 1 cup sugar. Spread over top of crescent rolls. Drizzle 1/2 stick of melted margarine over top of cream cheese mixture. Layer another package of crescent rolls crosswise over top. Mix 1 stick melted margarie, 1 cup of sugar and 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg. Pour over top. Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
