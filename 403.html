Butter Pecan Ice Cream

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-24 18:01:50

servings:

notes:

Barbara Hogue's recipe

recipe:

2 c. sugar 1 c. brown sugar 4 eggs 1 quart milk Cook until boils Add: 1 can pet milk 1 T vanilla 1 can eagle brand milk 1 t. butter flavoring 1 1/2 cups pecans toasted in Tbls oleo Put all together in ice cream maker. Add milk to the fill line. Churn.
