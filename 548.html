Apple-Oat Pancakes

author: ben_tilly

created: 2009-05-20 17:19:55

servings: 1.5

notes:

This started as a cooking experiment with my son. They are good served immediately, or eaten straight as a cold snack.

recipe:

1 cup applesauce 5/6 cups oat flour 1/2 cup milk 1 3/4 tsp baking powder 1/2 tsp salt 1/4 tsp cinnamon 1/8 tsp nutmeg Mix ingredients in a bowl. Cook pancakes. You should spread the batter out in the pan with a spoon.
