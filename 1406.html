Alain's Crepes

author: ben_tilly

created: 2014-05-11 20:11:52

servings: 2

notes:

I got this recipe from a co-worker named Alain.

recipe:

1/2 cup flour 1 cup milk 3 eggs dash of salt 1/4 tsp vanilla (optional) Mix, cook as very thin pancakes. Serve with desired toppings. For instance berries + whipped cream.
