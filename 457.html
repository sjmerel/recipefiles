Nettie's Meatloaf

author: gamegirl

created: 2008-01-16 03:10:16

servings: 6-8

notes:

recipe:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees Ingredients: 2 lbs lean ground beef, 1 lb bulk sausage, 1 handfull uncooked oatmeal, 1 egg, 1-2 T milk 1-2 T water 1-2 T Worchestershire sauce 1 pkg. dry onion soup mix Mix all ingredients well in a large bowl, adding more uncooked oatmeal a little at a time if it's too sticky. Place on the top of a roasting pan rack and shape into a long loaf. Cook in preheated oven for 1 1/2 hours or until juices run clear.
