Linen Towel

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-05-30 14:25:32

servings:

notes:

recipe:

3 eggs 1 cup sugar 1 teaspoon cinnamon 2/3 cup pumpkin 1 teaspoon salt 3/4 cup flour mix eggs, pumpkin, and sugar mix in dry ingredients pour onto lightly greased wax paper covering a 12 by 19 by 1 inch cookie sheet bake at 350 for 20 minutes invert wax paper onto a linen towel lightly dusted with powdered sugar peel off wax paper roll up the linen towel with the pumpkin mix refrigerate for 60 minutes make filling 8 oz. cream cheese, softened 1/4 cup butter 1 teaspoon vanilla 1 cup powdered sugar beat until smooth unroll linen towel spread filling over pumpkin roll up without towel
