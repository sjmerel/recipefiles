Salsa Chicken

author: Rick_Sandra

created: 2008-05-24 22:19:49

servings: as many as you want

notes:

Wayne and Wendy serve this

recipe:

Salsa Chicken 1 jar salsa 2-3 Tbsp brown sugar 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard Mix together, pour over chicken breasts or chicken pieces in casserole dish. Bake 350F for 45-55 minutes.
