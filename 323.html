Smoked Sausage

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-05-30 15:29:51

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 lb smoked sausage 1 medium green pepper cut into strips 1 medium sweet red pepper cut into strips 1 banana pepper seeded and cut 1 medium onion, sliced 1/2 cup beer 1 tablespoon prepared mustard cut sausage into 2 inch pieces and slash through skin combine sausage and veggies in covered skillet combine beer and mustard and pour into skillet bring to boil reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes
