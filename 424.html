Nonnie's Meatloaf

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-28 17:52:19

servings:

notes:

My boys love it.

recipe:

1 1/2 lbs ground chuck onion 2 eggs garlic powder parmesan cheese 1 8oz tomato sauce Knead together meat, eggs, garlic powder, parmesan cheese until forming a meat loaf. Use garlic and parmesan to your taste. I use lots of parmesan. Put in loaf pan. Pour tomato sauce over it. Cook 350 for an hour.
