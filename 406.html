Fudge Cookies

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-24 18:11:16

servings:

notes:

By Mary Lou Messersmith

recipe:

2 c. rolled oats 1/2 c coconut 1/3 cup cocoa 1 tsp vanilla 1/2 cup nuts Mix these ingredients in bowl. In saucepan put 2 cups sugar 1 stick oleo 1/2 cup evaporated milk Bring to a boil and boil 4 minutes. Pour over dry ingredients, beat hard and drop by spoonfuls on wax paper.
