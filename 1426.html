7-up Biscuits

author: texaskadi

created: 2014-06-29 11:46:56

servings: 6

notes:

ONLY 4 INGREDIENTS!!!!! Bisquick, Sour Cream, 7-Up and Butter!!! These are excellent!!!! So easy and soooo good!!!!

recipe:

INGREDIENTS: 4 cups Bisquick 1 cup sour cream 1 cup 7-up 1/2 cup melted butter DIRECTIONS: Mix Bisquick, sour cream and 7 up. Dough will be very soft - don't worry Knead and fold dough until coated with your baking mix. Pat dough out and cut biscuits using a round biscuit / cookie cutter. Melt butter in bottom of cookie sheet pan or 9x13 casserole dish. Place biscuits on top of melted butter and bake @ 425 degrees for 12-15 minutes or until brown
