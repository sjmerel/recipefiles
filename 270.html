Old-Fashion Cheesecake

author: ksvoboda

created: 2006-07-04 21:13:09

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 can crescent rolls 1 1/2 cup sugar 1 8 oz sour cream 3 8 oz. cream cheese 5 eggs 2 tsp. vanilla Mix all ingredients except eggs. Add the eggs (one at a time) and then the vanilla. PRess crescent rolls in 9" x 13" greased pan and up the sides of the pan. Pour batter; sprinkle with cinnamon on top. Bake at 375 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes.
