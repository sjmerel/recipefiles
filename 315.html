Cheesey Potatoes

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-05-30 13:52:23

servings:

notes:

recipe:

2 lb pkg frozen hash brown potatoes 8 oz. cream cheese 2 cans cream of celery soup 1 soup can full of milk 4 tablespoons instant onion 8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese mix all ingredients except cheese cook at 350 degrees for 45 minutes add cheese and cook until melted
