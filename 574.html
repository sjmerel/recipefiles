5 Layer Salad

author: jmderusha

created: 2009-11-15 22:14:31

servings:

notes:

recipe:

Layer: Head of lettuce, torn into bite size pieces 2 c. frozen peas 2 c. diced celery 1 c. sliced water chestnuts 1 c. chopped onion 8 hardboiled eggs, sliced 16 slices bacon, crumbled. Combine: 2 T sugar 2 c. mayonnaise Spread over top like frosting and sprinkle with grated cheese (cheddar) Cover with wrap and refrigerate overnite.
