Tuna Noodle Casserole

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-05-29 20:12:45

servings:

notes:

recipe:

2 cups macaroni noodles 1/4 cup margarine 1/4 cup chopped onion 1/4 cup green pepper 1/2 cup flour 1/2 teaspoon salt 2 cups milk 8 oz. cream cheese 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce 1 nine oz. can tuna breadcrumbs cook macaroni. Cook onions, green pepper, and margarine together until soft. Combine all ingredients. cook in 2 qt. casserole dish for 30-40 min at 375 degrees
