Barbecue Ribs

author: ksvoboda

created: 2006-07-04 20:27:54

servings:

notes:

recipe:

3 lbs. ribs 1 cup maple syrup 1 cup ketchup 1 cup chopped onions 1/4 cup brown sugar 1/2 cup water 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar 2 tblsp. olive oil 1 tblsp. worcestershire sauce 4 cloves garlic 1/2 tsp salt 1/2 tsp hot sauce 1 tblsp. lemon pepper Boil ribs and cover for 1 hour. Sprinkle with lemon pepper & marinate overnight with 1/2 of sauce. Grill next day and use rest of marinade for dipping.
