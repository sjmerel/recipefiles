Orange Jello Cake

author: jmderusha

created: 2009-11-15 21:39:22

servings:

notes:

350 degrees

recipe:

1 pk. 2 layer yellow cake mix 2 c. boiling water 1 c. orange juice 1 pk. 6 oz. orange jello Prepare cake mix as directed on back of box. in a 13 x 9" pan. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes Cool Cake for 15 minutes Then prick with utility fork at 1/2 " intervals. Meanwhile dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Add orange juice and pour over warm cake. Chill at least 2 hours and top with cool whip.
