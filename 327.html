Ribs

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-06-01 16:12:19

servings:

notes:

recipe:

cut ribs into pieces for BBQ cut 3 or 4 salt both sides with adolph's salt salt 1 side with onion salt, the other with garlic salt broil for 10 minutes on each side, or until brown but in roaster and almost cover with water, use drippings roast for 1.5 hours covered at 350 degrees
