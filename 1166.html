Brussels Sprouts roasted

author: Lorag123

created: 2012-10-19 18:23:13

servings: 4

notes:

recipe:

1 lb brussel sprouts Sprinkle with a little smoked paprika on oiled sprouts 1 TB olive oil 1/4 tsp salt Preheat oven to 375 Place sprouts on baking sheet and toss with oil and salt Roast untilmgolden brown and crisp on the outside but still soft and tender on the inside About an hour
