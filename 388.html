Guacamole Dip

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-22 20:12:26

servings:

notes:

2 ripe avocados 1 8oz. pkg. cream cheese (at room temperature) 1 small onion 1/2 cup sour cream or less 1 T. lemon juice

recipe:

Blend avacados and cream cheese in mixmaster. Then add other ingredients. Serve with corn chips
