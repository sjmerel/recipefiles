Bernise's Ceasar Salad

author: georgette

created: 2007-10-25 03:15:15

servings: 6

notes:

This is my daughter's recipe and she tells me you have to have all the right ingredients in order for it to turn out right.

recipe:

Ground black pepper [about 1 Tbs] more if you like it peppery 4 garlic buds, chopped fine 8 capers 4 egg yolks 1/4 cup olive oil 1/2 Tbs worcestershire sauce 1/2 Tsp red wine vinegar 1/2 Tsp hot mustard 1 tsp lemon juice couple dashes of Tabasco Serve on romaine lettuce and add fresh parmesan cheese.
