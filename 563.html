Bag of Dried bread pieces 1 can chicken broth 1 sm can sliced olives 1 c. chopped green and red bell peppers 1/2 c. dried cranberries 1/2 c. strained mandarin oranges *Grand Marnier Liquor 1 stock of chopped celery **1 cup of juice from Turkey 1/2 tsp. parsley 1/4 tsp basil 1/4 tsp. rosemary *soak cranberries and oranges in warm grand marnier for 1 hour or overnight in fridge. **Saute vegies in olive oil until semi-soft. Add bread pieces and broth Put in cassarole dish and sprinkle w/ seasonings Bake for 20 min. at 325
