"Greens" french toast

author: susanandrowell

created: 2005-02-25 03:04:56

servings: 4

notes:

From "Fields of Greens" by Annie Somerville

recipe:

4 eggs 1 c milk (I use whole) 2 T sugar zest of 1 orange, minced 1/2 tsp cinnamon (the recipe says "preferably fresh ground, yeah right, that's why I don't use this cookbook much) 8 slices challah or sourdough (although I've used soft wheat bread and it's good too) butter Beat eggs, milk, sugar, zest and cinnamon and put in a shallow dish. Soak the bread. Fry in butter until lightly brown.
