Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-06-04 15:32:09

servings:

notes:

Grandma Shantz's

recipe:

2 quarts milk 6 eggs 2 cans condensed milk 3 cups sugar 1 teaspoon salt 1 tablespoon vanilla heat milk in heavy pan in bowl mix eggs, 1 can condensed milk, sugar, and salt pour egg mix into hot milk and heat until mixture coats the spoon remove from heat stir in 2nd can condensed milk cool freeze
