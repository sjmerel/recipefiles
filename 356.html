Low Fat Butter Sauce

author: donnarae

created: 2007-07-14 18:53:16

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 c 1% milk 2 t cornstarch 3 T Molly McButter Wisk milk and cornstarch, heat until steaming and add Molly and bring to a boil, stir constantly and cooke a few min. Pour over veg, pot, pasta add shredded cheese for a good cheese sauce.
