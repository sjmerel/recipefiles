Mashed Potatoes

author: GrandmaP

created: 2009-08-20 17:32:30

servings:

notes:

Girl Scout cook book submitted by Ann Reichert

recipe:

Potato Buds (enough for 10-12 servings), 8 oz cream cheese, 1 small pkg french onion dip. Mix potatoes according to package directions leaving out 1/3 cup milk. again serving for 10-12. Beat cheese until smooth- add dip. Add to potatoes. Dot with butter and bake 350 for 1/2 hour.
