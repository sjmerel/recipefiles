Potato Salad

author: jmderusha

created: 2007-11-12 02:52:58

servings:

notes:

Very popular at work potlucks

recipe:

4 cups cooked potatoes, cut up into chucks 1/2 cup sweet onion, chopped 4 eggs, hard boiled, diced into chunks 1/2 cup chopped pickles, dill or sweet. (Sweet is preferred at our house) 1 cup Best Foods Mayonnaise 2 T. cider vineager 1 t. mustard 1 1/2 t. salt (if doubling this recipe, do not double the salt) 1/4 t. pepper 1 t. sugar Blend and chill.
