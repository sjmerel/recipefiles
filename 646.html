APPLE PIE

author: wendyp2010

created: 2010-11-23 08:53:44

servings: 6-8

notes:

recipe:

7-8 c sliced apples (toss w/2 TBS lemon juice, if desired 1 c sugar 1/4-1/2 c flour 1 t cinnamon, or more 1/4 t salt 1/8-1/4 t nutmeg 2 TBS butter, chopped Bake at 425 degrees for 15 mins, turn down to 350 degrees, bake for 40-45 mins cover crust edge with foil or pie crust cover
