Easy Shortribs

author: jmderusha

created: 2009-11-15 21:21:28

servings:

notes:

recipe:

4 carrots 4 Shortribs (Lean and bone in) 3 onions 1 pkg. dehyd. onion soup mix Turn oven to 250 degrees. Peel carrots and cut in half lengthwise Peel onions and cut in thick slice On abot 3 thickness of heavy duty foil, layer shtribs betweens between vegetables and sprinkle onion soup mix over all. Seal tightly Place on cookie sheet and bake 3 to 4 hours Turn oven off until ready to eat.
