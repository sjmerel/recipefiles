Homemade Chocolate ice cream

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-27 20:16:50

servings:

notes:

recipe:

5 eggs 2 cans eagle brand milk 1 1/2 pint whipping cream 1 c sugar 1 t vanilla 1 cup chocolate syrup Put eggs in blender and whip, add sugar and whipping cream and blend. Add the vanilla and syrup. Pour and add enough milk to the "fill" spot on your ice cream maker.
