Sour Cream Noodle

author: jmderusha

created: 2011-10-06 17:33:57

servings: 4-6

notes:

Brenda

recipe:

1 lb. ground beef 26 oz. jar spaghetti sauce 8 oz. elbow mac. uncooked 8 oz. sour cream Brown Hamburger in large skillet, drain. Add spaghetti sauce. Spread macaroni in a single layer over beef & sauce Cover and cook over med heat for 10 min. or until noodles are tender. Add water if necessary to prevent sticking. Add sour cream and mix. Heat through. Makes 4-6 servings.
