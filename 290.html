Broccoli Salad

author: lutucker

created: 2007-01-10 19:31:20

servings:

notes:

Judy H's

recipe:

1 1/2 lbs. broccoli-cut into bite size pieces 3/4 C chopped red onion 1 C grapes, cut in half 10 strips of bacon crumbled 3/4 C. sunflower seeds toasted Dressing: 1/2C Mayo 1/2C Miracle Whip 3/4 C. sugar 2 T. vinegar 1 T. milk Sir until smooth consistency. I would recommend making the dressing ahead ( at least a few hours). combine all ingred w/ dressing just before serving. (Can adjust the amount of dressing used - I only used 1/2)
