Taco Pizza

author: jmderusha

created: 2007-11-12 02:36:27

servings:

notes:

Family Favorite Very popluar at family get togethers

recipe:

With fork blend: 2 cups bisquick 1/2 cup water Knead 5 times or so and spread on greased pizza pan. Do not over knead it, the crust will get tough. Spread on top of this Bisquick dough crust: 1 medium size can refried beans Brown: 1 pound hamburger 1 pkg. Taco Seasoning 1/2 can diced green chilis Spread over the top of the refried beans. Bake for 20 mins. at 350 degrees or until crust is browned. Grate Longhorn cheese (or Mexican Cheese blend) over browned hamburger mixture and put back in oven until melted. Serve with grated or sliced taco style lettuce, diced tomatoes, diced olives and salsa/sour cream.
