Neiman Marcus Chocolate Chip Cookies

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-27 20:08:48

servings:

notes:

A lady asked for this recipe after eating one of these cookies at Neimans and had it charged to her card. She was told it cost 250. When she got her bill it was $250.00. So she passes this recipe to anyone she can. Enjoy!

recipe:

May be halved. 2 cups butter 4 cups flour 2 tsp baking soda 2 cups granulated sugar 2 cups brown sugar 5 cups blended oatmeal. Measure and blend in blender to a a fine powder 24 oz chocolate chips 1 tsp. salt 1 8 oz hershey bar (grated) 4 eggs 2 tsp baking powder 3 cups chopped nuts(Your choice) 2 tsp vanilla Cream butter and two sugars. Add eggs and vanilla; mix together with flour, oatmeal, salt, baking powder and soda. Add chocolate chips, Hershey Bar and nuts Roll into balls and place two inches apart on a cookie sheet. Bake for 10 minutes at 375. Mkes 112 cookies. Have fun!!! This is not a joke.
