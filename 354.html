Grapefruit Marguritas

author: donnarae

created: 2007-07-14 18:44:49

servings:

notes:

serve immed or freeze from Shirley very, very good

recipe:

6 oz limeade concentrate 2/3 c ruby red grapefruit juice 2/3 c white tequila 1/4 c orange liquer (triple sec) combine ingred in blender. add ice to 3 1/2 c level - process until ice is finely crushed.
