Hamburger Mac

author: jmderusha

created: 2011-10-06 17:55:01

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 1/2 lbs. hamburger 26 oz. jar spaghetti sauce 2 7 1/4 oz. boxes of macaroni and cheese 8 oz. pkg shredded mozzarella cheese Brown hamburger, add tomatos and tomato sauce Combine macaroni and ground beef mixture into an ungreased 13 x 9 baking dish. Sprinkle with moz. cheese Bake uncovered @ 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
