Crock Pot Cream Cheese Chicken Recipe

author: gentleduv47

created: 2007-04-15 15:54:45

servings:

notes:

I got this recipe from a nurse at work. We're always sharing easy and family-pleasing recipes. The sauce tastes like a creamy alfredo sauce. This is great with noodles. ...I got this recipe from the internet, and the family really likes it.

recipe:

3 lbs chicken pieces 1 (2/3 ounce) package Italian salad dressing mix 4 tablespoons melted butter (divided) 1 small onion, chopped 1 garlic clove, chopped 1 (10 1/2 ounce) can cream of chicken soup 8 ounces cream cheese 1/2 cup chicken broth 1. Place chicken pieces in crock pot and sprinkle Italian seasoning over chicken. Sprinkle with 2 tablespoons melted butter. 2. Cook on low for 4-6 hours. 3. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a sauce pan and saute onion and garlic. Add Cream of Chicken Soup, cream cheese, and chicken broth. Stir until smooth. 4. Add this mixture to crock pot and cook on low for an additional hour.
