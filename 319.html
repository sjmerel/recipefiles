Apple Cake

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-05-30 14:11:26

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1.25 cup oil 2 cup sugar 2 eggs 1 tablespoon vanilla 1 tablespoon cinnamon 4 cups peeled and sliced apples 3 cups flour 1 teaspoon salt 2 teaspoons baking soda mix oil, sugar, and eggs add all other ingredients bake in greased and floured 9 by 13 pan at 350 degrees for 60 minutes
