Sugar Cookies

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-29 14:32:48

servings:

notes:

From Margaret Edna Holiman, mom's friend

recipe:

1 cup oleo 1 cup sugar 1 cup powdered sugar 1 cup oil 1 tsp almond 1 tsp vanilla 4 cups flour 1 tsp soda 1 tsp cream of tartar salt Mix and chill overnight. Take pieces, roll in a ball and flatten with sugar. Bake 350 for 12 minutes
