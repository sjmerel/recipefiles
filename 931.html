Parmesan Chicken

author: jmderusha

created: 2011-10-06 17:50:07

servings: 4

notes:

Brenda

recipe:

1/2 c. mayo 1/3 c. grated Parmesan cheese garlic powder 4 thin bonelss chicken breasts 3/4 c. Italian Bread Crumbs In med. mixing bowl, combine mayo, cheese and garlic. Coat chicken with mayo mixture and cover each with bread crumbs. Arrange on an ungreased baking sheet. Bake 425 degrees for 15-20 minutes until lightly golden and juices run clear.
