Brian's Clam/corn chowder

author: barfirst

created: 2014-09-12 11:56:53

servings: 8

notes:

recipe:

1/4 lb salt pork (finely cubed) 3-4 med onions chopped 3 can minced clams 1 can evaporated milk water 1 small can corn kernels 1 large can crm style corn 5-6 med potatoes (cubed) saute pork until brown add onions saute until light brown cover with boiling water bring to boil - add potatoes cook until potatoes almost done lower heat to simmer add milk, clams, corn cook summer 10 min
