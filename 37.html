My Dad's Spanish Shrimp

author: sarahjane

created: 2004-07-19 15:39:07

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1/2 lb shrimp, peeled 3-4 cloves garlic, minced Coarse salt, to taste, (more is better) 2 tbs minced parsley tsp hot pepper flakes tbs or more paprika Extra virgin olive oil. Heat large frying pan or saute pan. Add three or four tbs olive oil and the garlic, saute for 30 second or so. Add shrimp, cook till they are nearing done and nicely turning pink. Add coarse salt and paprika and finally, the hot pepper flakes and parsley, stirring well. Serve immediately with chunks of fresh bread.
