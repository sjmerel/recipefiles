Flapjack

author: steveandsarah

created: 2007-09-23 21:52:01

servings:

notes:

Easiest... cookies... ever. From the Islington Cookbook

recipe:

4 oz brown sugar 4 oz butter (half butter and half canola oil also works) 4 oz oats 4 oz flour 2 Tbsp golden syrup (or honey or maple syrup) optional: 1/2 tsp vanilla optional: pinch of salt Melt butter in a bowl in the microwave; add brown sugar and mix. Add everything else. Spread on a baking tray and bake at 375 F for 10-20 minutes, until lightly browned. It will firm up as it cools. Try not to eat it all at once.
