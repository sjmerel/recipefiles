tuna casserole

author: donnarae

created: 2007-07-14 17:36:18

servings: 6

notes:

recipe:

1 box of kraft mac & cheese 1 4oz can mushrooms 1 can campbell mushroom soup 1 can French's onions prepare mac & cheese as directed - add 2 peices of American cheese add mushrooms mix soup with 1/2 can water, mix until smooth and add to mixture add 1/2 can of onions pour into a casserole and bake @ 350 for 30 min, add the remaining onions on top and more American cheese - bake until cheese is melted
