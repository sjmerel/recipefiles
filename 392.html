Shake well in jar or mix in blender the following ingredients:

recipe:

2 cups of Kraft Miracle Whip or mayonnaise 1cup ketchup 2/3 cup vinegar (white) 2 T. garlic powder 2 T. powdered oregano 1 T. paprika 3 T. worcestershire Refrigerate. Will keep indefinitely
