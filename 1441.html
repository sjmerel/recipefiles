Cheryl's Fancy Pants Chicken

author: texaskadi

created: 2014-08-17 13:09:34

servings: 6

notes:

recipe:

You'll need 2 packages of THIN chicken breasts a few slices of prosciutto a half bag of fresh spinach an 8 oz package of fresh sliced mushrooms 1 envelope Lipton Recipe Secret's Savory Herb and Garlic 1/2C White Wine 1/4C Olive oil 8oz shredded mozzarella Preheat oven to 375 spray a 13/9" pan with pam put 2 layers of chicken in pan, cover with proscuitto, top with spinach, sprinkle with a little kosher salt and coarse black pepper top with mushrooms whisk together olive oil, wine and savory herb envelope pour over everything. Lay a piece of foil over (not tight) bake for 30 minutes top with cheese and bake for 5 minutes more.
