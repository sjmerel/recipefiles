Four Bean Salad

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-06-04 15:16:22

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 16 oz. can green beans, drained 1 16 oz. can wax beans, drained 1 16 oz. can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed 1 16 oz. can kidney beans, drained and rinsed 1/4 cup green pepper chopped small 5 green onions chopped 3/4 cup sugar 1/2 cup cider vinegar 1/4 cup vegetable oil 1/2 teaspoon salt Mix all together best if let to settle overnight
