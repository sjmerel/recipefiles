Dutch Babies

author: ben_tilly

created: 2013-10-04 14:45:19

servings: 1-2

notes:

These are pure decadence. We usually serve with sugar and syrup sprinkled on top. Lemon is also supposed to be good. The recipe is fairly robust. Alter ingredients/proportions to whatever you like best.

recipe:

2 eggs 1/2 cup milk 1/2 cup flour 1 tbsp sugar 1/4 tsp salt 1/4 tsp vanilla 1/4 tsp cinnamon 1/8 tsp nutmeg 2 tbsp butter Put cast iron pan in oven. Preheat to 450 F. While that is warming up, mix everything but butter in a bowl. Melt butter in pan. Pour batter in pan, put it back in the oven, cook for 15 min at 400 F.
