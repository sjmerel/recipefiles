Waffles

author: goodrums

created: 2007-12-01 15:04:12

servings: 4

notes:

Given with waffle iron for Michael and Jeanne

recipe:

2 cups of flour 1/2 cup sugar 4 tablespoons baking powder 1/4 t. salt 1 egg 1 cup cream 1 cup milk 1/4 cup melted butter Beat egg slowly. Add cream. Add milk. Add dry ingredients (sifted together) Add butter. These are very thick due to the large amount of baking powder, but make a very crisp waffle.
