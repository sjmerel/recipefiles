Molasses Cookies

author: gretchenlewis

created: 2009-11-22 00:41:45

servings:

notes:

recipe:

3/4 c butter 1 c sugar 1 egg 1/4 c molasses 2 c flour 2 t soda 1/2 t cloves, salt, ginger 1 t cinnamon Melt butter, cool Add sugar, eggs and molasses; beat well. Sift flour and spices Mix together well Chill Roll into 1" balls Roll in sugar Grease cookie sheet Bake in 375 oven for 9-10 minutes
