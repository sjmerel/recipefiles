Cherry Walnut Cake

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-05-30 14:09:23

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 lb. butter 1 lb powdered sugar 6 eggs 3 cups flour 1 cup chopped walnuts 1 10 oz. jar cherries (cut in halves) Mix all ingredients together, will be thick plop into greased bundt pan bake at 300 degrees for 90 minutes sprinkle powdered sugar over top after cooled
