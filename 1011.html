Hash Brown Potatoes

author: myrnawis

created: 2012-01-24 11:23:45

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 bag hash brown potatoes with peppers 1 can cheddar cheese soup 1 can cream of chicken 1 8oz sour cream 1/2 stick butter Stir above ingredients into the potatoes. Top with 2 cups corn flakes 1/2 stick melted butter bake 350 for 45 min
