Ground Beef Casserole

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-27 16:31:21

servings:

notes:

recipe:

2 green peppers chopped 4 onions 4 cloves crushed 1/4 cup salad oil 4 cans tomato soup 2 cans tomato paste 4 t. salt pepper 2 cans whole kernal corn juice of 1 lime 2 large cans of mushrooms(drained) Reserve juice 2 12 on. spaghetti 4 lbs ground beef 1 1/2 lbs American cheese Saute first three in oil. Add soup and paste. Season. Let simmer Add corn, juice of lime and mushrooms. Boil spaghetti and drain. Brown meat in mushroom juice Layer: Corn, spghetti, and meat Sprinkle each layer with cheese Bake 350 for 45 minutes....
