Broccoli Salad

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-06-04 15:20:00

servings:

notes:

recipe:

2 bunches broccoli- flowerets only 6-8 strips cooked bacon, crumbled 1/2 cup golden raisons 1/2 cup sunflower kernels 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese 1 small purple onion, chopped combine all ingredients and toss Dressing: 1 cup mayonnaise 2 tablespoons vinegar 1/2 cup sugar stir together, make sure sugar is dissolved pour dressing over salad and toss right before serving
