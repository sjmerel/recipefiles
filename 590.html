Cowboy Caviar

author: gretchenlewis

created: 2009-11-22 00:57:42

servings:

notes:

Combine first 10 ingredients and let marinate for 1 hour Spread cream cheese on plate Pour marinated beans and olives over cream cheese Top with eggs and green onions Serve with tortilla chips

recipe:

16 oz can black beans 16 oz can black olived, chopped 1 onion, chopped 1 clove garlic, minced 2 T vegetable oil 2 T lime juice 1/4 tsp salt 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper 1/4 tsp cumin 1/8 tsp pepper 8 oz cream cheese, softened 1 hard boiled egg, chopped 1 oz green onions, chopped
