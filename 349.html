stuffed peppers

author: donnarae

created: 2007-07-14 17:39:16

servings: 4

notes:

recipe:

2 large peppers (green, yellow or red) 3/4 lb grnd beef,browned and drained 1 pkg min rice, cooked 1 lrg jar of Ragu Spag sauce mozerella cheese mix all and stuff in peppers - bake at 350 for 30 min, add more cheese to top and bake until cheese is melted
