Corn Bread with Cheese and Jalepenos

author: georgette

created: 2007-10-21 05:55:41

servings: 8

notes:

My own version using packaged corn bread mix

recipe:

2 packets of corn bread mix- get the sweet kind 2 eggs 2/3 cup milk 1/3 cup vegetable oil 1/2 cup finely chopped onions 1 [14 oz ] can of cream corn 1 cup grated cheddar cheese 6-8 jalepeno slices from a jar - chopped Oven at 350 for 35 minutes Pan 8x8 or 9x9 greased with crisco Pour the corn bread mix in a bowl, add the eggs and milk. Use a whisk to mix, don't overmix. Add oil and mix well, add chopped onions, can of corn, grated cheese and jalepenos. Pour in pan and bake for 35 minutes or until done. You'll never make cornbread any other way!
