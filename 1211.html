Tex Mex Cornbread

author: Lorag123

created: 2012-10-30 17:08:36

servings: 6

notes:

recipe:

1 can cream style corn 1cup yellow cornmeal 2eggs 1tsp. Salt 1/2 tsp. Baking soda 3/4 C milk 1/2 C melted butter 1 can (4 oz) sweet green chillier, drained &'chopped 1/2 C (2 oz) grated sharpmcheddar cheese 2 .TB butter Preheat oven to 400. In bowl mix corn, meal, eggs, salt, soda, milk, butter & chillies Add 1/2 of cheese. Place bitter in a 9 inchnskillet and place in oven to melt Pour in corn mixture and sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake for another 40minites or until crusty and golden brown Cut in wedges
