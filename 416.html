Broccoli Casserole

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-25 18:26:01

servings: 8

notes:

by Jimmie Cox

recipe:

2 10 oz frozen broccoli 1 cup grated American or mild cheese 1 can cream of mushroom soup 1 small can pet milk 1 3 1/2 oz. can French fried onion rings Prepare broccoli by cooking until just thawed. Drain and place in 2 quart casserole. Sprinkle cheese over top, combine soup and milk. Pour mixture over broccoli cheese mixture. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes. Remove and top with onion rings. Return to overn for 8-10 minutes or until onion rings are crisp and brown.
