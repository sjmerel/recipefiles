Bacon Quiche

author: RESABET

created: 2014-10-16 22:27:35

servings: 4 to 6

notes:

Donna

recipe:

Pie Pastry 1/4 cup green onions 1 Tbsp Butter 6 Eggs 1 1/2 cup whipping cream 1/4 cup unsweetened apple juice 1 lb. sliced bacon - cooked & crumbled 1/8 tsp salt 1/8 tsp pepper 2 cups shredded Swiss Cheese Wisk eggs, cream, apple juice. Add bacon, salt, pepper, & onions Swiss Cheese on top. Bake 350 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes.
