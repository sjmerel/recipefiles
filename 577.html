Pimento Potatoes

author: gretchenlewis

created: 2009-11-21 16:40:46

servings: 8

notes:

Melt butter; blend with flour. Add salt, pepper and onion. Gradually add milk and cook until thick. Remove from heat. Add cheese and blend well. Add pimento and parsley. Place a layer of potatoes in buttered baking dish and half of the sauce. Add the remaining potatoes and sauce. Bake at 375 for 45 minutes, or until nicely browned.

recipe:

4 cups cubed cooked potatoes 4 tbsp. butter 4 tbsp flour 1 1/2 tsp salt 1/4 tsp.pepper 2 cups grated cheese 2tbsp. chopped pimiento 2 tbsp.chopped onion 2 tbsp. chopped parsley 2 cups milk
