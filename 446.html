Lasagna

author: jmderusha

created: 2007-11-14 03:13:39

servings:

notes:

very good

recipe:

1 1/2 pounds hamburger 1 pkg. Lasagna Noodles 1 small (3 to 4 oz.) can tomato sauce 1 reg. size Hunts tomato sauce with tomato bits diced onion to your taste garlic salt or powder to your taste 1 T. basil leaves 2 T. parsley flakes Pint cottage cheese 2 eggs, beaten 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated 2 cups Mozzarella cheese Brown hamburger with some garlic salt, or powder and onion. Pour in both cans tomato sauce and simmer for 30 minutes. While simmering, boil lasagna noodles according to back of pkg. directions. Mix: cottage cheese, spices, parmesan cheese and 2 beaten eggs. In oblong pan layer: 1/2 Lasagna noodles 1/2 cottage cheese mixture 1/2 hamburger sauce mixture 1/2 mozzarella cheese repeat Bake 350 degrees 45 minutes
