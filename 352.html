Creamy Almond Dressing

author: donnarae

created: 2007-07-14 18:41:36

servings: 6

notes:

from Sam's Club can be prepared and refrigerated up to 1 week

recipe:

6 T blanced almonds, chopped 6 T water 1/2 c plus 2T fresh lemon juice 2 T honey 1/2 t salt 1/2 T fresh basil, minced 1/4 t dill 1/4 t lemon zest 1 canola oil putalmonds and water into blender and run on high to puree. Add remainig ingred, except for oil and run to blen and whip thoroughly. with motor running, slowly pour in oil and run until the dressing is thick and creamy
