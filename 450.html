Grandma Seviers Hamburger Sauce over Rice

author: jmderusha

created: 2007-11-20 14:57:25

servings:

notes:

My grandma has been gone since 1986, but this recipe stands as one of our all time favorites. Quick and easy. AND CHEAP...

recipe:

1 to 1 1/2 pounds extra lean hamburger/ground beef 1/2 large onion, diced 1 can tomato soup 1 can mushroom soup Rice Brown hamburger and onion. Add soups Blend and stir until hot over medium heat Make Rice of your choice. Serve sauce mix over the rice. Mmmm Good! I double this recipe for my family
