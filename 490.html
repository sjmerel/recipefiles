recipe:

1 lb chicken breast, cooked, cut up into bitesize pieces, See chicken reciepe 6 cups spinanch leaves 1/2 cup red radishes sliced 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds Dressing 2 tbsp olive oil 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar 1 tbsp soy sauce 1 tsp sesame oil In a small bowl whisk together dressing. and put on chicken and let marinate for at the least 15 min but up to a couple hours. In a large bowel combine spinach, radishes and sesame seeds Poor chicken mixture over spinach. The chicken mixture will look like it will have alot of liquid, thats ok use it like a dressing and mix togather.
