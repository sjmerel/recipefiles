Dog Bones

author: gretchenlewis

created: 2009-11-22 01:15:08

servings:

notes:

Preheat oven to 350. Combine flour, oats, baking powder. Set aside Mix milk, peanut butter and molasses until smooth Knead together. Will be stiff. Roll to 1/4" thick, cut to shapes Bake for 20 minutes

recipe:

3 c wheat flour 1/2 c oats 2 t baking powder 1 1/2 c milk 1 1/4 c peanut butter 1 T molasses
