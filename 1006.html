Italian Zucchini Saute

author: myrnawis

created: 2012-01-24 11:06:43

servings:

notes:

recipe:

3 Tbls butter 3 c. zucchini cut in 1/8" slices 1/4 c chopped onion 1/2 tsp basil 1/4 tsp all season salt 1/8 tsp. ground black pepper 1 med cubed tomato 3/4 c shredded mozzarella cheese 1/3 c parmesan cheese Melt butter. Stir in Zucchini, onion, basil, salt and pepper. Cook stirring occasionally, about 10-12 min. until zucchini is crisply tender. Stir in tomato - cook 1 minute. Sprinkle with mozzarella & parmasan cheese. Cover let stnd 1 minute or until cheese is melted. Freezes well.
