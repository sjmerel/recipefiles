Mom's Duck Recipe

author: goodrums

created: 2007-10-24 16:45:48

servings:

notes:

recipe:

Put apple and onion in cavity of the duck Crisscross bacon on breasts. Put in pan. Cover with 1 can boullion, 1 can red cooking wine. Cook 2 hours at 325 degrees, basting every so often. Let cool. Strip meat off bones and soak in gravy before serving
