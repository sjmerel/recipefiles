Warm Taco Dip

author: jmderusha

created: 2007-11-11 23:53:04

servings:

notes:

Good Holiday Party Crock Pot Recipe

recipe:

1 pound hamburger, browned and drained Add: 1 can refried beans 1 pkg. Taco Seasoning Put in slow cooker. Cover that with: 1/2 bottle Taco Sauce Cover that with: 1 cup sour cream Cover that with: 1 cup shredded cheddar Cheese Top that with: sliced black olives Serve with Taco Chips.
