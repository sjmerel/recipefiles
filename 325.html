Cabbage Rolls

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-06-01 16:00:24

servings:

notes:

recipe:

1 cup uncooked rice 1 large head cabbage 1 egg 1 medium sized onion, chopped and sauteed 2 lbs hamburger (or 1lb veal and 1 lb pork) 1 can tomato soup 1 can tomato sauce 2 tablespoons butter salt and pepper cook rice and cabbage mix meat, rice, egg, onion, salt, and pepper fill up leafy side of cabbage leaf with mixture roll with stem ending up on outside line pan or roaster with any cabbage remaining put rolls in roaster combine soup and sauce pour over rolls put butter on top bake covered for about 2 hours
