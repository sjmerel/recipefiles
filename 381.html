Shrimp Spread

author: georgette

created: 2007-10-21 06:48:42

servings: 12+

notes:

My sister Mona came up with this concoction and she has to make it for every party. It's a family favorite!

recipe:

1 8 oz package of cream cheese 2 Tbs Ketchup 3 Tbs of French salad dressing 1/4 cup of finely chopped onions 1/4 cup of mayo or Miracle Whip 1 can of shrimp rinsed and well drained Mix all together and pass the crackers!
