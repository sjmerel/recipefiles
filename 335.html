Zucchini Nut Bread

author: omgdarcie

created: 2007-06-04 15:37:35

servings:

notes:

recipe:

3 cups sifted flour 1.5 teaspoon cinnamon 1 teaspoon baking soda 1/4 teaspoon baking powder 1 teaspoon salt 3 eggs 1.75 cups sugar 1 cup oil 1 tablespoon vanilla 2 cups grated unprepared zucchini 1/2 cup chopped walnuts 1 teaspoon flour sift together 3 cups flour, cinnamon, soda, salt, and powder beat eggs well gradually add sugar and oil to eggs add vanilla and sifted dry ingredients stir in zucchini combine walnuts and flour and stir into mixture pour into 2 greased loaf pans bake at 350 for 1 hour cool for 10 minutes wrap tightly and let mellow overnight
