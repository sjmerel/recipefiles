Couscous Salad

author: gretchenlewis

created: 2009-11-21 16:36:21

servings: 8

notes:

cook couscous according to box directions (I cook in chicken broth) add remaining ingredients, chill and serve

recipe:

1 box couscous 1 c chopped tomatoes, seeded 1/4 c chopped basil 1/2 c olive oil 1/4 c balsamic vinegar 1/4 tsp red pepper
