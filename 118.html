Williamsburg Cheese Straws

author: caglepc

created: 2004-10-17 17:24:14

servings:

notes:

from Southern Living magazine, years ago

recipe:

Preheat oven 350 degrees Mix dry ingredients: 1 c. flour 1/2 t. salt 1/4 t. dry mustard 1/8 t. cayenne cut in with pastry blender or two knives: 1/3 c. butter 1/2 c. grated sharp cheddar Add 1 1/2 T. ice water Roll out thin on floured board with rolling pin. Sprinkle with another 1/2 c. cheese. Fold over in thirds, so that the cheese is covered. Roll out this dough again, then fold in thirds as before. Roll and fold again. Roll thin (c. 1/4"). Cut in strips. Place on cookie sheet. Sprinkle with celery seeds Bake 12 min.
