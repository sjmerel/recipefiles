Fresh Tomato & Avocado Salad

author: LadyT

created: 2007-08-21 01:48:55

servings: 4

notes:

Delicious taste of summer. Pair with the French Onion Chicken for a yummy summer dinner. Great in wraps or on focaccia sandwiches.

recipe:

Gather: 4 roma tomatoes- cut up to small cubes 1 clove of garlic-minced 1 vidala onion-sliced into small bits and sauteed in olive oil 1 avocado-cubed sea salt pepper herbs* 2 handfuls-boston lettuce-wash/dry/break into bite size pieces italian dressing add fresh basil if you have some Then: ~Combine-toss-enjoy! *I use Aldi's spice club seasoning w/ grinder -Country Herbs- ingredients: coarse sea salt, rosemary, parsley, oregano, basil, mint, sage, thyme
